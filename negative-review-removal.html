FAQ

Negative Review Removal FAQ

Simple answers to common questions people ask before they request help with Google reviews, Reddit threads, Ambition Box reviews, and Quora posts.

Can we delete GMB reviews?

You cannot directly delete a Google Business Profile review unless you wrote it. But if the review is fake, spam, abusive, off-topic, or breaks Google policy, it can be reported for removal.

Can we remove bad Google business reviews?

Yes, some bad Google business reviews can be removed if they break Google rules. Real customer reviews are harder to remove, so we also help brands respond, improve trust signals, and reduce the damage.

Can we delete negative threads from Reddit?

Some Reddit posts or comments can be removed if they break subreddit rules or Reddit policy. If removal is not possible, we can help reduce the impact by building stronger positive and helpful brand signals.

How do negative reviews hurt business?

Negative reviews can lower trust, reduce leads, affect sales calls, hurt hiring, and make buyers choose competitors. They can also appear in Google search results and influence what AI tools say about your brand.

How long does negative review removal take?

It depends on the platform and the type of review. Some cases move fast, while others need evidence, follow-up, and multiple review requests. We first check the removal chance before giving a clear plan.

Can Ambition Box reviews be removed?

Ambition Box reviews may be removed if they break platform rules or include harmful, fake, abusive, or private information. If removal is not possible, we help improve employer reputation signals around the brand.
