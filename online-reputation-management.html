FAQ

Online Reputation Management FAQ

Simple answers to common questions founders ask before they start online reputation management.

What are online reputation management services?

Online reputation management services help brands monitor, improve, and protect how they appear in reviews, search results, Reddit, Quora, forums, social platforms, and AI search tools.

Can online reputation management remove negative reviews?

Some negative reviews can be removed if they are fake, spam, abusive, private, or break platform rules. If removal is not possible, ORM can help reduce the damage with better responses and stronger trust signals.

How do I improve my brand reputation online?

You can improve your brand reputation by fixing review gaps, answering public questions, building helpful content, responding to complaints, strengthening founder authority, and tracking brand mentions across key platforms.

Does online reputation affect AI search results?

Yes. AI search tools may use public reviews, discussions, mentions, and trusted sources to understand a brand. Weak or negative reputation signals can affect how your brand is described or recommended.

How long does online reputation management take?

The timeline depends on the size of the problem, the platforms involved, and how many reputation gaps need work. Some fixes can happen quickly, while stronger reputation building usually takes consistent work.

Why do founders need online reputation management?

Founders need online reputation management because buyers, investors, candidates, and AI tools often check public trust signals before they contact the company. A weak reputation can quietly reduce leads and trust.
